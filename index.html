Do You Like Soup?

What kind of soup do you like?

My favorite is clam chowder. Because, well...CHAO-der.

So let's find the best canned clam chowder in SF.


  • Boudin Classic San Francisco bread bowl, a favorite for my family (as you can see below).
  • Hog Island Reddit praises their clam chowder, so does every famous chef ever.
  • Bar Crudo Broth-forward and briny, not just a creamy overload. Beloved by Bay Area locals for their fresh clams.
  • Tadich Grill An Old School Classic. Grandpa's favorite (soudough included obviously).
  • Cioppino's Obviously get the cioppiono, but the clam chowder is just as good.
